Detective Challenge – Student Questionnaires
This repository contains student responses to a science education activity called Detective Challenge. The goal of the activity was to engage students in critical thinking and scientific reasoning by analyzing a biological mystery.

Files
Original_questionnaire_portuguese.tsv
Contains the original student responses in Portuguese, collected during the Detective Challenge activity.

Translated_questionnaire_and_analysis.tsv
Includes the translated responses (Portuguese to English) along with preliminary analyses and categorization of the answers.

> Groups
	The participating students were from the following institutions:

	VRB – Colégio Estadual Visconde do Rio Branco

	CEFET – Centro Federal de Educação Tecnológica, UnEd Petrópolis

	NP – Colégio Estadual Nilo Peçanha
